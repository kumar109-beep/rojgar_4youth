About Mera Rojgar

Now-a-days the Indian youth want to be self-employed but nobody is available to show the right path. The Youth Rural Entrepreneur Foundation’s “Mera Rozgar” scheme aims to make the youth self-reliant by giving them employment. The aim of “Mera Rozgar” is to be a guide the youth, teach them and help them to reach the right destination. We also take care of the likes, dislikes and beliefs of every person, if a man cannot work with chicken, cannot work with fish, or doesn’t want to do farming, we will take responsibility of the best solution. The Center for Excellence guarantees employment. People in every village buy goods, which is the basis of every human. People drink tea, buy vegetables, take a haircut, buy clothes, buy household goods etc. The city/town economy is taking advantage of the village economy. Our foundation’s plan is that, if the economy of the village stays in the village, then the financial status of the village can improve dramatically.

In the “Mera Rozgar” scheme, we are focusing on smart street vendors where cycle rickshaws, e-rickshaws, mobile carts on bicycles are built and we will provide a moving shop & the entire facility for installation at a very cheap price. We will link these schemes with the government scheme, so that the youth can easily finance himself with our help. With this the youth can easily take a bicycle or e-rickshaw cart wherever he desires and can open up a barbershop, tea-snack shop, clothes shop, barber shop, vegetable etc. on a bicycle or a cart.
